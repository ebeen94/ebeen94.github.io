Analyzing the rental properties in the Greater Toronto Area with REATLOR.ca data

  • GitHub

DATA

I collected the data from REALTOR.ca based on the search of rental properties in the Greater Toronto Area. I crawled the data on 2022/03/11 including the address, bedrooms, bathrooms, area code, monthly rental price, parking spaces, property types and public remarks

I did an extra labeling job on the collected data since there were many properties that were located in the basement but did not have specific tags. I thought it was important to have this information because living in a full apartment alone is very different from living in a bedroom or a basement of another personâ€™s house. So I labeled the data based on the housing type: 1) bedroom, 2)basement, 3)upper-unit, 4)(whole) apartment by reading public remarks column, which contains a short paragraph of extra information about the house including the housing type, facilities and electricity fees, etc.

After that, I removed data with property types of vacant land, parking space, or offices, to only look at living properties. Data without address or duplicates were also removed. Then, I used a total of 501 data for the analysis.
